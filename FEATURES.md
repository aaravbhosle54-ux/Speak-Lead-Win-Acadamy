### 📝 Speech Outline Generator

#### Speech Types

**1. Persuasive Speech**
- **Purpose**: Convince your audience to adopt your viewpoint
- **Structure**:
  - Hook and credibility establishment
  - Main arguments with supporting evidence
  - Address counterarguments
  - Strong call to action
- **Best For**: Arguments, recommendations, proposals

**2. Informative Speech**
- **Purpose**: Educate your audience about a topic
- **Structure**:
  - Attention-grabbing introduction
  - Background information
  - Main points with examples
  - Analysis and significance
  - Summary and resources
- **Best For**: Reports, educational presentations, explanations

**3. Storytelling Speech**
- **Purpose**: Engage through narrative and emotion
- **Structure**:
  - Scene setting and character introduction
  - Exposition and context
  - Rising action and conflict
  - Climactic moment
  - Resolution and reflection
- **Best For**: Personal experiences, case studies, inspiring stories

**4. Motivational Speech**
- **Purpose**: Inspire and empower your audience
- **Structure**:
  - Inspiring opening hook
  - Identify challenges
  - Share personal story and journey
  - Provide actionable steps
  - Call to action and empowerment
- **Best For**: Team building, encouragement, inspiration

**5. Impromptu/Toastmasters Style**
- **Purpose**: Quick, structured speaking
- **Structure**:
  - Brief acknowledgment (5-10 seconds)
  - Clear thesis (10-15 seconds)
  - Supporting points with examples (30-45 seconds)
  - Conclusion and thanks (10-15 seconds)
- **Best For**: Unexpected speaking opportunities, rapid responses
